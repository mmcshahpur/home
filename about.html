Founder :  Fr. Nicolas J. Pollard (SJ)
Established :  1947 (74 Years Old)

St Mary Magdalene Church is a Roman Catholic church in Shahpur under the Diocese of Buxar (Bihar). There is an old church here from years ago. The current church building is relatively new. On the wall behind the altar, there is a large portrait depicting the resurrected Jesus Christ meeting Mary Magdalene. The high tower of the church is architecturally most beautiful even from a distance. This church has been serving the local community for a very long time. Church festivals are celebrated here with great enthusiasm and devotion.

There is a grotto in front of the church.
